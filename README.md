# Privacy Policy

**Roast Helper** — Last updated: March 24, 2026

## The short version

Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

## What data the app uses

Roast Helper stores your roast logs, bean details, and app preferences **locally on your device**. This data never leaves your phone.

## What we don't do

- We don't collect personal information
- We don't track your usage or behavior
- We don't use analytics or advertising SDKs
- We don't send your data to any server
- We don't share or sell data to third parties

## Permissions

The app does not request access to your camera, location, contacts, or any other sensitive device features.

## Third-party services

The app does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
